About Us

Flowery Days is a florist located in the heart of Dublin city, since opening our doors in 1985, we've been dedicated to providing high quality, fresh and beautiful flowers for any occations.
We have diverse lineups for our bouquets and flower collections which are freshly picked locally, as well as subscription service that adds colors to your daily life.

Why Choose Us?

Luxurious freshly cut flowers

We are committed to using locally-sourced flowers whenever possible. We have carefully designed our lavish hand-tied bouquets to ensure the recipients wow at our flowers.

Fast Free Delivery

We have developed an effective and fast delivery network which brings the flowers throughout the whole of Ireland. All the flowers can be sent for next-day delivery.

Vast Range of Products

Our vast range of flowers can cover any occasions such as birthday flowers, thank you flowers etc. We also design bespoke flower arrangements for venues such as for weddings and company events.
